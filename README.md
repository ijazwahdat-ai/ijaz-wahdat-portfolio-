##  About Me

Hi, I'm **Ijazulhaq Wahdat**, an AI Engineer based in Kabul. I specialize in **Large Language Models (LLMs)**, **Generative AI**, and **Enterprise Automation**.

My primary focus is modernizing the telecommunications sector through intelligent automation. Recently, I led a significant R&D initiative at the **Ministry of Communications and IT (MCIT)**, engineering Afghanistan’s first domain-specific AI Agent capable of understanding complex telecom queries for AWCC, Salaam, and Roshan.

##  Featured Projects

### 1. [Afghan Telecom LLaMA (MCIT)](https://github.com/ijazwahdat-ai/Afghan-Telecom-LLaMA-MCIT)
*A Domain-Specific LLM for Afghanistan's Telecom Sector.*
- **Tech:** Llama-2-7B, QLoRA, PyTorch, Hugging Face.
- **Achievement:** Achieved **89% factual accuracy** in handling USSD codes and ISP policies.
- **Impact:** Solved the hallucination problem in generic models for local telecom data.

*AI Assistant with Pashto, Dari, and English Support.*
